## Election Results 2023: BJP Wins in 3 Hindi Heartland States, Congress Wins in Telangana

Summary | 
:---:|
![](/assets/images/2023-12-04-India_News_Headline_231204_1-1.webp) |
The BJP is poised for victory in Madhya Pradesh, Rajasthan, and Chhattisgarh, while the Congress is leading in Telangana. The elections for these states were held in November. The BJP is credited with its strong performance to the leadership of Prime Minister Narendra Modi. The Congress victory in Telangana marks the end of Chief Minister K Chandrasekhar Rao's reign. Turnout for the elections was high, and the results are seen as a reflection of the people's faith in Modi's development agenda. |

## Rajasthan Election Results: BJP Wins 115 Seats, Congress Bags 69

Summary | 
:---:|
![](/assets/images/2023-12-04-India_News_Headline_231204_1-3.webp) |
The BJP has emerged as the winner in the Rajasthan Assembly election, winning 115 out of 199 seats and set to form the new government. This result continues the trend of voting out the incumbent party in the state. The Congress, which won the state in 2018, has managed to secure only 69 seats. Various key politicians including Vasundhara Raje, Sachin Pilot, and Diya Kumari have won their respective seats. Ashok Gehlot has conceded defeat and is expected to resign as Chief Minister. The BJP's victory has been attributed to PM Modi's leadership and the party's development agenda. |

## Telangana Election Results: Congress Emerges Victorious

Summary | 
:---:|
![](/assets/images/2023-12-04-India_News_Headline_231204_1-4.webp) |
The Congress party has emerged victorious in the Telangana Assembly election, winning 64 out of 119 seats. The ruling Bharat Rashtra Samithi (BRS) won 39 seats, while the BJP secured 9 seats. AIMIM, led by Asaduddin Owaisi, won all 7 seats it contested. The Congress victory marks the end of the BRS' rule in Telangana. Telangana Congress chief Revanth Reddy is expected to become the new Chief Minister. The election results were announced along with those of Rajasthan, Madhya Pradesh, and Chhattisgarh. The Congress has accepted its defeat in these states and vowed to rebuild. |

## Chhattisgarh Election Results 2023: BJP Wins, Bhupesh Baghel Resigns

Summary | 
:---:|
![](/assets/images/2023-12-04-India_News_Headline_231204_1-5.webp) |
The Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) is set to reclaim power in Chhattisgarh, winning 54 out of 90 seats in the state assembly, while the ruling Congress party got 35 seats. This marks a stunning comeback for the BJP, who were voted out in the previous 2018 polls. Bhupesh Baghel, the current Chief Minister of Chhattisgarh, has met with the governor and tendered his resignation following the defeat of the Congress. The official declaration of the results is awaited. The BJP's victory is attributed to Prime Minister Modi's welfare policies and the hard work of party workers. |
